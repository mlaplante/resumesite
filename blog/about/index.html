About

Michael LaPlante

SVP of Information Security and Operations

I'm a technology executive with over 15 years of experience spanning information security, engineering leadership, and software development. I currently serve as SVP of Information Security and Operations at Proforma, where I oversee security strategy, infrastructure, and engineering teams.

Before moving into security leadership, I spent years as a hands-on engineer building web applications, leading development teams, and teaching others to code. That engineering background shapes how I approach security — pragmatically, with an emphasis on building secure systems rather than bolting security on after the fact.

This blog covers what I'm thinking about: security trends, engineering practices, and the tools I use to build things. Sometimes I write about the process itself — how AI-assisted development is changing the way I work.
